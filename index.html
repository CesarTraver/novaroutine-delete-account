Account & Data
Deletion Request

You can request the complete deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how and what is deleted.

What data we store

  • Google account identity — your name and email address obtained via Google Sign-In (OAuth 2.0).
  • Google Drive access — if you granted Drive permission, we may read or write files within the app's designated Drive folder. No other Drive content is accessed.
  • Google Calendar access — if you granted Calendar permission, we may read or create events associated with your in-app activity. No other calendar data is accessed.
  • In-app activity data — game progress, scores, and settings stored locally on your device and/or synced to our backend.
ℹ We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties for advertising purposes. Google Play Services data is handled in accordance with Google's own privacy policies.

Confirmation & deletion

We will confirm receipt within 5 business days and complete the deletion process within 30 days of your request.

What gets deleted

  • Account identity data — name, email, and Google user ID removed from our systems.
  • In-app activity data — all game progress, scores, and preferences stored on our servers.
  • Drive-linked files — any files created by the app in your Google Drive within the app's designated folder. Files you created manually are never touched.
  • Calendar-linked events — any calendar events created by the app on your behalf.
  • Local device data — cached data on your device is removed the next time you open the app after deletion, or you can clear it manually from your device's app settings.
⚠ Revoking app permissions from your Google Account does not automatically delete data stored on our servers. You must submit a deletion request to ensure complete removal.

Deletion timeline

Confirmation Within 5 business days of receiving your request.
Full deletion Within 30 days of your request. Backups are purged within the following backup cycle.
Legal retention Certain transaction or compliance records may be retained for up to 90 days as required by applicable law.

Delete in-app data yourself

Most of your data is stored directly on your device and can be deleted without contacting us. This includes routines, history, settings, and locally cached content.

How to delete your data from the app →

Revoking Google permissions

In addition to submitting a deletion request, you can revoke the app's Google Drive and Calendar permissions at any time from your Google Account security settings.

Visit myaccount.google.com/permissions, find Nova Routine in the list, and click Remove Access. This revokes OAuth tokens immediately.
