Account & Data Deletion
Nova Routine

Delete Your
App Data

Most of your data lives directly on your device. You can delete it yourself in seconds — no need to contact us.

Delete from the app

1

Open Settings

Tap the settings icon in the main navigation of Nova Routine.

2

Tap "Delete data"

You will find this option at the bottom of the Settings screen.

Settings › Delete data
3

Confirm deletion

A confirmation prompt will appear. Once confirmed, all local data is permanently removed from your device. This action cannot be undone.

⚠ This action is irreversible. Make sure you no longer need your routines, history, or settings before proceeding.

Data we cannot delete for you

Some data may exist outside our systems — in your own Google Drive or in files you have exported. We have no access to those copies and cannot delete them on your behalf.

Google Drive backups

If you enabled Drive backup inside the app, Nova Routine saved a copy of your data to your personal Google Drive account. That file belongs entirely to you — we cannot access or delete it.

To delete it, open drive.google.com, find the Nova Routine folder, and delete its contents from there. You can also do this from the Google Drive app on your device.

.csv

CSV export

Spreadsheet files exported from the app are saved to your device storage or wherever you chose to save them. Delete them as you would any other file.

.json

JSON export

Raw data exports are saved locally or shared to apps of your choice. We have no record of where these files ended up after export.

.pdf

PDF reports

PDF documents generated in the app may have been saved to your device, printed, or shared via email or other services. You are responsible for deleting those copies.

ℹ If you need help locating exported files on your device, check your Downloads folder or the app (e.g. Files, Google Files) used to save them at export time.

Need more help?
